# NutriLens Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** April 2026

NutriLens (“we”, “the app”) respects your privacy. This policy describes how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

## Information we use (on your device and through Apple / Google)

- **Camera & photos:** Used only to scan barcodes and analyze meal photos you choose to capture or select. Images are processed to provide nutrition insights inside the app.
- **Motion & fitness (step count):** Used to read your daily step count (via Apple’s motion sensors) to show activity on the home screen and estimate calories burned from walking—for example, displaying “8,240 steps today” with an approximate kcal value.
- **Purchases:** Subscription status is processed through Apple App Store (and RevenueCat as a subscription helper). We do not receive your full payment card details.
- **Advertising (where applicable):** Our ad partners may use identifiers allowed by the platform to show ads to non‑subscribers, in line with their policies.

## Data storage and sharing

- Core nutrition and usage data you enter are primarily stored on your device unless you use features that require a network (e.g. AI or cloud analysis)—in which case only what is needed for that feature is sent over an encrypted connection.
- We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with service providers (e.g. subscription, analytics, ads) strictly to operate the app, as permitted by law.

## Your choices

- You can revoke camera, photo library, or motion access anytime in **Settings → NutriLens** on iOS.
- You can manage or cancel subscriptions in **App Store → Account → Subscriptions**.

## Contact

For privacy questions, contact us at the support email or form listed on your App Store listing (update this line with your real contact once published).

## Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
